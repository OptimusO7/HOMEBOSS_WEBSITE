Optimus Technologies was founded with a vision to provide IT services and technology solutions. In a world where technology evolves rapidly, we saw an opportunity to create a unified, intelligent system that truly understands and adapts to homeowners' needs.

HomeBoss emerged from countless hours of research, development, and a deep understanding of what modern homeowners desire: simplicity, security, and smart automation that just works.
